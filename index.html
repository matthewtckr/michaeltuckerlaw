“Those who fail to plan, plan to fail!” – Source Unknown

Medicaid Planning is an important issue that affects not only the elderly and infirm, but their children as well. Many seniors find that their previous planning is inadequate when it comes to Medicaid and long term care. Many thousands of dollars are lost to families each and every day because of inadequate planning or no planning at all. It goes without saying that it’s never too early to plan ahead.

I. Michael Tucker understands the fears and anxiety that comes with long term care issues. My personal involvement came as a result of his own medical circumstances that pointed in the direction his practice would become. Just as for others he realized that senior issues would become part of his life as well as the lives of his clients.

Mr. Tucker is a Central Florida estate planning attorney with over 32 years of experience in the areas of estate planning, probate, and medicaid planning who throughly believes in helping his clients succeed.

Mr. Tucker’s estate planning tools includes revocable living trusts, irrevocable living trusts, charitable trust planning, private (family) foundations and a wide variety of planning tools to eliminate probate..

In addition to the tools mentioned above, Mr. Tucker is also involved in creating new businesses through the use of partnership agreements including family limited partnerships, limited liability companies and corporations. He is also involved in business succession planning through the use of buy-sell agreements, installment sales agreements, private annuities, etc.
